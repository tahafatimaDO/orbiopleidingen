VCA Basis Spaans Cursus

Your employer and clients want work to be done safely, so that no one is harmed. With a B-VCA diploma in your pocket, you know what is expected of you in the workplace. It is also important for employers so that they know that you are complying with the rules. Content of the VCA Basic course

 Legislation
 Risks and prevention
 Hazardous Substances
 Fire and explosion
 Work at height
 Confined spaces
 Tools and machines
 PPE

What can you expect?

After following the course you are fully aware of all basic knowledge, as stated by VCA. Before the exam, your knowledge will be tested by means of a mock exam.
